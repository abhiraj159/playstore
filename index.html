About this game

Welcome to PokerBaazi, India’s Biggest Poker Platform.

Where you can Instantly Withdraw your funds and enjoy a plethora of exclusive features.

Do you love Poker? Do you want to personalize the way you play Poker? Then you’ll be joining 30 Lac+ users who answered yes to both the questions.

PokerBaazi is a platform focused on taking your Poker playing experience to the next level.

If you are just starting out, learn how to play Poker and test your skills on Freerolls and Practice games for free and get a chance to win from a monthly prize pool of 25 LAC+.

PokerBaazi offers classic Poker formats such as:

  • Texas Hold ‘Em
  • 4 Card Omaha
  • 5 Card Omaha
  • 6 Card Omaha

PokerBaazi offers monthly tournaments with a combined prize pool of 30 CR+ where real players all over India, come and take part to show off their Poker prowess.

PokerBaazi has been the first to introduce recreational Poker formats in India such as:

  • All-In or Fold
  • Fast
  • Sit & Go
  • And many more to come ….

Why you will love the PokerBaazi experience:

  • Instant Withdrawals
  • Custom Table Themes
  • Emojis & Throwables
  • In-app text/voice chat
  • RNG Certified

Features to help improve your game:

  • Player stats
  • Hand History
  • Player Notes
  • And much more …

Our marquee events such as EndBoss, G.O.A.T (Greatest of All Tournaments), National Poker Series and much more give you an opportunity to win from the biggest prize pools in India.

All of this culminates into making PokerBaazi a fun, entertaining and a rewarding Poker platform.

This app is for 18+ audiences, all over India, except Telangana, Sikkim, Orissa, Assam and Indian states where games of skill are not allowed.
